PISCES: Adaptive Importance Sampling Methods for Bayesian Inference in Complex Systems (JCJC-ANR 2018-2021)

In this project, we research novel adaptive IS-based methods for probabilistic inference in complex non-linear high-dimensional systems. We push the adaptive IS (AIS) methodology so it can be applied to intricate realistic complex systems, achieving a high performance, adjusting automatically the required complexity, and still attaining solid theoretical guarantees that we will also analyze.
